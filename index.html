A Town of History & Nature

Welcome to Bad Belzig, a state-approved spa town nestled in the scenic High Fläming Nature Park. Our town offers a unique blend of historical charm, natural beauty, and modern relaxation.

From the mighty Eisenhardt Castle to the soothing thermal baths of the SteinTherme, Bad Belzig is a place of discovery. Explore our picturesque old town with its historic market square and charming alleys.
