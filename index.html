Discover Bad Belzig

The historic spa town in the heart of the High Fläming Nature Park.

Your Spa Town in the Fläming

Welcome to Bad Belzig, a state-approved spa town with a history stretching back over a thousand years. Nestled in the scenic High Fläming Nature Park, our town offers a unique blend of historical charm and natural beauty.

From the mighty Eisenhardt Castle to the soothing thermal baths of the SteinTherme, Bad Belzig is a place of relaxation and discovery. Explore our picturesque old town with its historic market square, St. Mary's Church, and charming alleys.

Experience & Explore

SteinTherme Thermal Bath

Relax and rejuvenate in our famous saltwater thermal spa. Enjoy various pools, saunas, and wellness treatments.

High Fläming Nature Park

Hike or cycle through pristine forests, rolling hills, and idyllic landscapes. Discover the region's unique flora and fauna.

Culture & History

Explore Eisenhardt Castle, visit local museums, and walk through the medieval town center to experience our rich heritage.

Hiking & Nature

The High Fläming is a paradise for hikers. Discover well-marked trails that lead you through stunning natural landscapes, past historical sites, and to breathtaking viewpoints.

Art Trail (Kunstwanderweg)

A unique trail combining nature with art installations. This circular route offers surprising and thought-provoking sculptures along the way.

Panther Trail (Panther-Rundweg)

This trail takes you around the historic town center, offering fantastic views of Eisenhardt Castle and the surrounding landscape.

European Long-Distance Path E11

Part of a larger European network, this trail passes through the Fläming, connecting diverse landscapes and cultural highlights.
